# ccleste

C/SDL port of Celeste classic built for Symbian S60 v3.

# Added features:

* Auto save/load state when closing/opening the game.
* Ability to take screenshot.
* Ability to disable/enable music.
* Ability to adjust audio volume.


# Installation

1) download [cceleste_gcce.sisx](https://github.com/JigokuMaster/ccleste/releases) 

2) you may need to install PIPS 1.7

# Controls

|Symbian Keys      |Action                               |
|------------------|-------------------------------------|
|LEFT              | Move left                           |
|RIGHT             | Move right                          |
|DOWN              | Look down                           |
|UP                | Look up                             |
|5/ENTER           | Jump                                |
|7                 | Dash                                |
|LEFT SOFT         | Pause                               |
|Hold 9            | Reset                               |
|1                 | Toggle screenshake                  |
|0                 | Turn off/on music                   |
|HASH/ASTERISK     | Adjust audio volume                 |
|GREEN             | Take screenshot (saved in C:\\data) |
